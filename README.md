# 🎮 KuttyHack 2.0 

## What is KuttyHack?

KuttyHack is a bi-weekly builder session where makers come together to build and ship projects over a weekend. The initiative aims to foster a strong maker culture by encouraging participants to learn through building, experiment with new technologies, and share their work publicly.

Every edition focuses on a specific theme or technology, challenging participants to turn ideas into working products within a short timeframe.

---

## This Week's Theme: Godot

For this edition of KuttyHack, participants are expected to build and deploy a simple game using the Godot game engine over the weekend.

The focus is not on creating a perfect game but on learning, experimenting, and most importantly, shipping something playable.

Deploy your game on itch.io and share it with the community.
